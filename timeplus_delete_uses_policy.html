Account and Data Deletion Request

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Time Plus, please follow the steps below. This process is managed by the developer, jacky.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to jenty521@naver.com with your account details and a request for account and data deletion.
  2. Within 24 hours, you will receive a confirmation email asking if you really wish to delete your account.
  3. Reply to the confirmation email to confirm that you want your account to be permanently deleted.
  4. Once your confirmation is received, your account and all associated data, including any premium subscription information, will be deleted within 24 hours.

Important Information

  • All data associated with your account, including premium subscription information, will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • The entire deletion process may take up to 48 hours from your initial request, but your account will be deleted within 24 hours after your final confirmation.
  • If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at jenty521@naver.com.
