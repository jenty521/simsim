About Quick Memo Pad

Quick Memo Pad is a fast, lightweight note-taking app that works right in your browser. No account needed, your notes are saved locally on your device.

Features:

  • Create unlimited sticky notes
  • Markdown support for rich formatting
  • Auto-save functionality
  • Lock notes for read-only mode
  • Easy sharing options
  • Works offline
